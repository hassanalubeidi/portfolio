NSP Merchandise—Ruby on Rails ecommerce site.

NSP Merchandise is a company that sells high-quality promotional merchandise and is based in Norwich, UK.

They approached me with the intention of creating a custom price calculator. I set about creating it in AngularJS, and when I showed them the calculator, they loved it and wanted to build an entire website around it.

Snippet of the calculator

I enjoyed creating the shop from scratch. It entailed the complex use of the session as well as automated Rails mailing, which is something I'd never used in a project before this. I also built a simple backend so that prices and contact details can be manipulated as well as allowing orders be tracked.

They make some really cool stuff btw, check out their instagram.
